# Privacy Policy for Forget Me Not

**Last Updated: January 6, 2025**

## Introduction

Forget Me Not ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

Our app collects location data to provide location-based reminder functionality:

- **Foreground Location:** We access your location when the app is open to help you select reminder locations and detect when you arrive at saved places.
- **Background Location:** With your permission, we access your location in the background to notify you of reminders when you arrive at specified locations, even when the app is closed.

**Important:** Location data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Reminder Data

We store your reminder information (task text, location names, addresses, and coordinates) locally on your device using secure storage. This data is not uploaded to any external servers.

### Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Premium features, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status from the app store.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide location-based reminder notifications
- Store your reminders locally on your device
- Enable premium features for subscribers
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All reminder and location data is stored **locally on your device**
- We use industry-standard encryption for local data storage
- We do not transmit your personal data to external servers
- We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

- Used to search for locations and businesses
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### RevenueCat

- Subject to [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### Expo / React Native
- Development framework for the app
- Subject to [Expo's Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Location Permissions
You can control location permissions through your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings > Apps > Forget Me Not > Permissions > Location

### Data Deletion
You can delete all app data by:
- Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)
- Clearing app data in device settings

### Opt-Out
You can opt out of location tracking by denying location permissions, though this will limit app functionality.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
